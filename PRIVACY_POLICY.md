# Privacy Policy for Underline

**Last Updated: January 26, 2026**

## Introduction

Underline ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension designed to help users learn languages through AI-powered word lookups and vocabulary management. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. User-Provided Information
- **API Keys**: Your OpenAI API key is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We never transmit your API key to any server other than OpenAI's official API endpoints.
- **Vocabulary Data**: Words you save to your vocabulary list are stored locally in your browser.
- **Settings**: Your preferences (such as custom Base URL and model selection) are stored locally.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Selected Text**: When you use the lookup feature, the selected text is sent to OpenAI's API for translation and definition. This data is not stored on any of our servers.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide AI-powered word definitions and translations via OpenAI's API
- To maintain your personal vocabulary list locally
- To remember your settings and preferences

## Data Storage

All user data is stored **locally** in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not operate any external servers to collect or store your data.

## Third-Party Services

This extension uses the **OpenAI API** to provide AI-powered translations and definitions. When you use the lookup feature:
- Your selected text is sent to OpenAI's servers
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this data: https://openai.com/privacy

## Data Security

- Your API key and vocabulary data are stored locally and encrypted by Chrome's storage system
- We do not have access to your stored data
- All communications with OpenAI use HTTPS encryption

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all stored data by removing the extension
- View your stored vocabulary through the extension interface
- Modify or delete individual vocabulary entries at any time

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the extension update notes.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please create an issue on our GitHub repository.

**Summary**: Underline stores all your data locally in your browser. We only send your selected text to OpenAI's API for translation services. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information on external servers.
